MISSION

The SCIS, as a unified digital platform for Zamboanga City, promotes the rights, welfare, and benefits of senior citizens by ensuring secure, efficient, and transparent management of their information. It strengthens collaboration among local government offices, service providers, and communities by providing accurate data for the formulation, implementation, and monitoring of localized policies, plans, and programs. The SCIS is dedicated to advancing active, productive, and healthy aging while fostering an enabling environment for older persons in Zamboanga City.

VISION

The Senior Citizen Information System (SCIS) envisions Zamboanga City as a model community where senior citizens enjoy accessible services, protected rights, and an improved quality of life through a centralized and secure digital platform. By equipping local government and communities with accurate and timely information, SCIS aspires to create an inclusive city that values, supports, and empowers older persons for active, healthy, and dignified aging.

STARCODE CORPORATION

The Senior Citizen Information System (SCIS) was developed by Starcode Incorporation to provide a secure, efficient, and centralized platform for managing senior citizen information in Zamboanga City. This system is designed to streamline registration, improve data accuracy, and enhance service delivery for our elderly community. By empowering local government offices and stakeholders with reliable data, SCIS helps ensure that senior citizens receive the care, benefits, and support they rightfully deserve.

GOAL

The Senior Citizen Information System (SCIS) aims to provide a fast and efficient registration process for senior citizens in Zamboanga City by shifting to a centralized digital platform. It seeks to ensure accuracy of records through proper verification, eliminating duplication and errors, while improving accessibility of information for both beneficiaries and service providers. SCIS is designed to enhance the delivery of government services and benefits to senior citizens, support evidence-based decision-making for local policymakers, and promote inclusion by upholding the rights, welfare, and dignity of older persons. Ultimately, the system strives to create a more responsive and reliable framework that empowers senior citizens and strengthens the services dedicated to their well-being.
